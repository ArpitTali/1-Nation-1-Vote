What's 1 Nation 1 Vote

1 Nation 1 Vote is an Application/Website developed by Arpit Tali through which you could vote your candidate easily by just sitting at your homes and using 1 Nation 1 Vote's facilities



What are the advantages of it?

  • We can vote from anywhere even patients can vote from hospitals.
  • It’s just safe like our net-banking/government applications/websites.
  • It requires finger-print, OTP, etc to open.
  • It would be connected with Aadhaar by which a person is able to give 1 vote. It would stop 2-3 vote problems.
  • No-one could force you to vote.
  • Vote freely and with freedom.
